## panoramic
- A wide, unobstructed view of a large area in all directions.

## dentoalveolar part of the radiographs
- Dentoalveolar (adjective) refers to the teeth and the surrounding alveolar bone (the part of the jawbone that holds the teeth).
> Related to the teeth (dento-) and the tooth socket area in the jaw (-alveolar).

## Dental Enumeration means:
- Identifying and numbering each tooth in a dental radiograph.

## An Orthopantomogram (OPG)
- is a panoramic dental X-ray that shows the entire mouth in one single image, including:
    - Upper jaw
    - Lower jaw
    - Teeth
    - Jaw joints
    - Surrounding bone structures

## Mesiodistal axial inclination refers to:
- The tilting or angulation of a tooth along the mesial (toward the midline of the dental arch) and distal (away from the midline) direction.
